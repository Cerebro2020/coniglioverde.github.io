FELICE VINO


Born in Potenza, I’ve graduated with honors in Foreign Languages and Literature. Over time I’ve experienced several areas of work and creativity - communication, storytelling, visual, novel, theatre, comic -. These experiences took me to video making. As owner of a video production company I’ve realized video clips, spots, corporate and I’ve written, directed and produced a zero serial of a sci-fi web series. I moved to Bologna in 2016 to work in a TV production company specialized in documentary and promotion of the territory. There I’ve designed, written and directed a TV format and a feature film.

FABIO VOLPI (a.k.a. dies_)


was born in 1971, formed at first as a visual graphic and then started to study architecture in milan and at the university epfl of lausanne in switzerland, and obtaining his degree in 1998. from 2001, he became member of otolab collective, participating in the creation of multimedia events that will be performed live in milan, rome, bologna, brussels, paris, mexico city, oslo, berlin, toulouse, tunis and montreal. In 2003 he found echran, an electronic-experimental noise band, and record 2 albums “echran”(ebria records) and “in offret” for the label a silent place. as member of the duo au+ (electro/ jazz/industrial)
