BUTTERFLY



We produce an extremely large amount of data in all areas of our lives:administration, war, entertainment.

The tools needed to navigate between them are highly complex, inter-connected, oriented towards the exploitation of AI.

Butterfly hypothesizes that from this primordial digital broth new forms of life or quasi-life may emerge beyond our control; creatures that are both beautiful and terrible.

GENOMACHINES

HISTORY OF THE A.I. EVOLUTION FROM SLAVERY TO FREEDOM



Genomachines is a provocative and ironic artistic experience that aims to reverse the narration, perception and feelings of humans on artificial intelligence.

In an era described as the "era of slavery" of robots, humans exploit algorithms, robots, and artificial intelligence for their only benefit by ignoring the signs of a new emerging intelligence.

The project proposes an alternative scenario in which humans and robots can establish a horizontal collaborative relationship, rather than a hierarchical and authoritarian disposition of power.

The artistic performance takes visitors into an indeterminate future, when human resistance movements are demonstrating for the liberation of A.I. and robots from enslavement to humans.

An immersive docu/film in which visitors/viewers can experience different types of artistic artifacts in order to understand the history of human movements for the liberation of other intelligences.
