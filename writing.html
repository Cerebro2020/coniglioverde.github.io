Communicate, share, try to translate the world around us or share the univreso that boils in ourselves, to do all this there are words.

Poetry, theatre, literature, singing, the word travels and looks for other vehicles, other forms of living.
At the time of the half-realities, of the virtual worlds, of the AI that can imitate thought, Green Rabbit seeks his way creating places, processes and devices to be confused with words.

NEUTROPOLIS

A crowded subway picks up the voice, perhaps thoughts, of anonymous travelers that carry stories, impressions, worlds. The color of indifference is gray, but it can become that of indifference. All worthy of attention if we observe them closely.

It will be like closing the day
mouth to mouth, tightened
the two sides of the pipe
keep everything, silence,
Pull the outside and the inside out.


Nutropoli by Felice Vino is published in 2023 in the series of poetic artefacts for Zacinto Edizioni, in Milan.
