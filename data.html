H.E.Arts stands for High Emotional Arts, a device based on the "Geneva Wheel" and designed to record the emotional journey of a visitor to a museum, art gallery or other types of artistic experience.

H.E.Arts transform the visitor emotional journey into a personalized and creative data visualization. Has been developed during Innocult program, thanks to MEET Digital Culture Centre contribution.

The prototype of this page uses a selection of works from the 900 Museum of Milan.

You will find the selected works in the app interface with a list of emotions that you can select, grouped into four key areas. Take your time, look at the art works and "let feel yourself".

Select one, two or up to three choices to describe the nuances of your emotions
Drag the image to go to the next work and so on to the last one.
Select the "result" button to see how your personal data create a unique work, the result of your personal experience.

A first visualization is designed to create a space to explore H.E.Arts_01, also in VR.

A second visualization creates a unique object, inspired by one of the artists in the museum H.E.Arts_02.

If you like you, you can use our contacts to tell us about your experience.
