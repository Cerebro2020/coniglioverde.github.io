H.E.Arts

H.E.Arts si the acronym fot High Emotional Arts a device designed to record the emotional journey of a visitor to a museum, gallery or other artistic experience based on the "Geneva Wheel".
It can select the type of emotion he feels to describe his experience which translates in a creative and personal data visualization.
The first prototype on this page sees the user recording their emotions on a selection of works from the Museo del 900 in Milan.

Take your time, look at the paintings and "let yourself to feel".
Pay attention to your emotions more than what the paintings are supposed to mean.
What matters is what you feel.

There is a list of choices in our interface, you can choose one, two or three to describe what your emotions are.
After choosing the first, slide your finger over the image to go to the next work and so on until the end.
At this point select the "result" button and you will be able to see how your personal data creates a unique work resulting from your personal experience.

At the end of data collection, which you can end at any time, you will be able to check the result.
