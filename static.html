EMBRYO

During its development cycle, the human embryo traces the evolutionary history: cell, fish, reptile, mammal.
In the series of digital elaborations, reinterpreting that journey in the light of scientific discoveries and cultural reflections.
The embryos of possible creatures demonstrate the consequences of an opaque evolution, the result of biological-alchemical overlapping and synthesis of the extended realms of living made up of animated and non-animated.

NOSTALGHIA

This AI project is inspired by Tarkovsky’s work. The galleries feature surreal creatures, hybrids of human and non-human.
They explore a deserted planet, perhaps a land long abandoned, where life is almost absent, a place where the ghosts of our future are already memories.

E-SCAPES

In this series of digital prints it’s explored the possibility of adopting mathematical syntax in composing poetic-scientific visual maps. Working with generative graphics software, basic geometric figures are subjected to tensive forces until they reveal protein-like archetypes that recall the mind - such as memory and imaginative drive - life forms, objects and landscapes. Environments and phenomena, digital and digitized, with which we interact and that act on us in an ever deeper way.
