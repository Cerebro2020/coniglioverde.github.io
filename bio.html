FELICE VINO


Born in Potenza, I graduated in Foreign Languages and Literature, over the years I have experimented with different forms of expression, writing, video, digital art.

I made video clips, commercials, corporate, the zero series of a web series of science fiction. I wrote, directed and produced, traveled, changed home and life, Potenza, Rome, Bologna, Milan.

Poetry suggested the path, cinema showed it to me, today I work with AI, data, CGI and with any tool I return that sense of wonder in front of the forms that can take the beautiful and the unknown.

FABIO VOLPI (a.k.a. dies_)


Music Author, Multidisciplinary Collaborator



Visual Artist and Sound Designer with a background in architecture. He was a member of the Otolab collective and founder of the band Echran. His skills range from creating multimedia events to teaching representation theory and sound design. He collaborates with several contemporary music collectives.

Tra le sue opere: “AURAE” (installazione audiovisiva, 2023), “LINESCAPE” (Video Mapping, 2023), “DOUBLE_SCAN” (performance+installazione audiovisiva, 2023), “EVANISHING POINTS” (Video Mapping, 2022), e “SLICE OF LIFE” (Video mapping, 2021).

MARILINA CIACO


Author texts for poetic vitual environments, content editor.


Writer and literary critic, she holds a Ph.D. in Contemporary Literature, Visual and Media Studies.
Actually collaborates with the University of Bologna and University of Zurich. Her main research fields are contemporary poetry and experimental writing.

Among her works: "Gli anni del disincanto" (2023), "Ghost Track" (2022) and "Intermezzo e altre sinapsi" (2020). Her critical articles have appeared in numerous literary magazines and blogs.
